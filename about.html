I grew up wanting to be an astronomer, and now I am one! I studied astronomy and music at Penn State, and got my PhD in astronomy from the University of Maryland, College Park. I moved to Milwaukee to join the CGCA at the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee for my first postdoc. I enjoy reading, playing violin, riding horses, doing science outreach, and spending time family and pets.
